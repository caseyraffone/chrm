# CHRM 72-Hour Finance Interview Reps Test

## Goal

Validate whether finance students will choose a packaged CHRM workflow over free-form ChatGPT prompting.

Primary signal: 20 early-access replies or emails in 72 hours.

Strong signal: 5 people say they would pay $29-$39 for recruiting-season access.

Kill/reshape signal: fewer than 5 serious replies after posting to warm channels and 2-3 relevant communities.

## Test URL

Local route: `/finance-interview-prep`

Production route after deploy: `https://chrm-two.vercel.app/finance-interview-prep`

## Core Pitch

CHRM is voice-first finance interview practice. It asks realistic IB/PE/markets/consulting questions, grades your spoken answer for technical correctness and delivery, then gives you the tighter version to record again.

## Decision Rule

Build more if:

- 20+ early-access emails/replies, or
- 5+ people explicitly say they would pay $29-$39, or
- 3+ people ask to use it for an upcoming interview this week.

Pause/reshape if:

- People say “cool” but do not click/reply.
- Students like the idea only when it is free.
- Users want content packs or answer banks more than voice grading.

## Next Product Changes If Test Passes

1. Make finance technical correctness the hero in App Store copy.
2. Add the “Redo this answer now” loop after feedback.
3. Consider replacing monthly-first pricing with a recruiting season pass.
4. Add a lightweight early-user onboarding path for target role and recruiting timeline.
